Who Are We?

NexGen is a student lead organization that strives to develop vital skills in students wishing to participate in competitions such as hackathons, science fairs, etc. NexGen specializes in providing mentorship to students who either don't have project ideas but aspirations to build one, or to students who have existing project ideas but have trouble implementing them. We provide personalized assistance through the various stages of competition including design, creation, and presentation. Our mentorship is broken down into 6 different tracks. These tracks are formed with the purpose of providing students with the full experience needed to excel in stem competitions.

Our Mission

As a competition-oriented club, we strive to supply students with the tools they need to build their dream projects. We have three main objectives. We aspire to help prepare students for competitions such as hackathons, science fairs, and innovation challenges, provide students with the technical experience needed to create any projects they would like to and not be constrained by technological ability, and host our own hackathons to help entice more students to get involved in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math(STEM). We hope that members of our program gain the tools they need to build their own projects, succeed in competition, and build a future career path in STEM.

Tracks

Our tracks are specialized to help students succeed in STEM-based competitions. They can be broken down into 4 sections: Computer Science, Human Skills, Engineering, CAD, and Development. We primarily provide students with technological tools while also teaching them important real world skills.

CS

Python and ML

Python is a powerful high-level programming language that can be used in a plethora of fields and is easy to learn and good for beginners. Our python track allows students to learn to implement Python into machine learning, and apply techniques to future projects.

Human Skills

Presenting

Presenting is a critical part of competitions and a useful life skill, and our presenting track trains students to unlock their full potential as a public speaker and a communicator.

Engineering

CAD

CAD is a prevailing tool for engineering and design in all industries. Gaining mastery of this skill will give students a unique tool in their skill set and give them an edge both in competitions and the real word.

Development

App and Web Development

App and web development are an excellent set of tools for students to present or supplement their projects. With these tools, they will be able to unlock their projects’ full potential.
