NexGen strives to be a community where like-minded individuals gather to innovate and devise the solutions of tomorrow.

What do you get out of NexGen?

Bring your ideas to life and execute in competitions with quality & experienced support.

1:1 Mentorship

Whether you're one student or part of a group, NexGen has a volunteer base of mentors to support you with any issue or roadblocks you hit on your course

Competition Prep

Nexgen also provides extensives databases of competitions that students may compete in. These may be recommended as per the project you are working on ensuring you the best chances of succes.

General Curriculum

Not only do we have specific mentorship/help, if you are not exactly sure where to start, we have general curriculum encompassing app/web development, machine learning, pubic speaking, presentation creating skills and more! .
